## Sensitivity Review

The SchoolCity screenshots extracted from the master DOCX include visible user identifiers and assessment/item content. To avoid distributing that content from this repository, the local `schoolcity-*.png` extracts were removed and are not embedded by the report. SchoolCity behavior and visuals are cited through the master DOCX metadata and the public support-source URLs below.

The Learnosity visuals are live screenshots captured from a local Pieoneer Learnosity Items API preview (assess mode) running in development/test mode on localhost. They intentionally show the rendered Learnosity demo item, the assess toolbar, and the item reference, and are cropped to the preview shell. Because this Confluence space is internal-only, no redaction was applied to the Learnosity captures.
